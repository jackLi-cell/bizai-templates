FAQ

How to use these templates?

After selecting a template, copy the prompt, paste it into your preferred AI tool (such as ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini), replace the information in curly braces, and generate your copy.

Is it free?

All templates on this site are completely free to use. You just need access to an AI chat tool.

Can AI-generated content be published directly?

We do not recommend publishing directly. Please review using the checklist included with each template before use.

Which AI tools are supported?

Any AI chat tool works, including ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot, and others.
