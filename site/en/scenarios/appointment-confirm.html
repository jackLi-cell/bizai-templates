Appointment Confirmation Dialogue

Confirmation communication and important notes when customers book services

Customer
I would like to book a hair appointment this Saturday afternoon. Do you have availability?
Recommended ReplyA（确认+引导选择）
Saturday afternoon is available! What time works for you? 2pm and 4pm are both open. Also, what service are you looking for - haircut, perm/color, or treatment? I will reserve the appropriate time for you.
Recommended ReplyB（时间紧张时）
Saturday afternoon currently only has the 4pm slot available - 2pm is fully booked. Does 4pm work for you? If not, Sunday morning also has openings.
Not Recommended
Yes, just come in.
Reason: 信息不完整，没有确认具体时间和项目，容易造成到店后等待
Customer
4pm works. I want to get a perm, roughly how much would that cost?
Recommended Reply（确认预约+告知信息）
Great, I have booked you for Saturday at 4pm for a perm. Depending on hair length and products used, the price ranges from 280-480 yuan. Our stylist Tony will give you specific recommendations based on your hair type and desired look when you arrive. A perm takes about 3 hours, so please plan accordingly. No need to wash your hair before coming in.

The above is a reference framework. Please adjust according to your actual situation and store policies.
