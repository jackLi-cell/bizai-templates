Refund Negotiation Dialogue

How to negotiate and reach a mutually acceptable solution when customers request refunds

Customer
I want a refund. This item looks nothing like the pictures - the color difference is huge.
Recommended ReplyA（先确认问题）
Sorry for the trouble. Could you send me a photo of the actual item? Let me check if we sent the wrong color. If there is indeed an issue, we can process a return or exchange.
Recommended ReplyB（直接给方案）
Sorry about that. Screen displays can indeed cause color differences. We support 7-day no-reason returns - I will send you the return address and we will cover shipping. If you prefer not to return it, we can also offer a 10 yuan discount as compensation. Which would you prefer?
Not Recommended
Our product page states that color differences are normal and refunds are not supported.
Reason: 虽然可能合规，但态度强硬容易引发投诉升级
Customer
I do not want to return it - too much hassle. But you need to compensate me.
Recommended Reply（给出合理补偿）
Understood, returns can be a hassle. How about this - we will refund 10 yuan to your account balance that you can use on your next purchase. We will also give you a 5 yuan off coupon for orders over 50 yuan as a gesture of goodwill.

The above is a reference framework. Please adjust according to your actual situation and store policies.
