General Customer Follow-up

Retail & E-commerce Customer Service Replies

Customers who inquired but did not purchase generate follow-upscripts

Use Cases

Use for after customer inquiry without purchase , after quotation without response

Not Suitable For

Not suitable for customers who have clearly declined

Materials Needed

  • customer name
  • content
  • on times communicate time
  • follow up

Prompt (Click to Copy)

[Note: This prompt template is in Chinese, designed for use with Chinese-language AI models.]

你是一位销售顾问。请为以下客户撰写跟进消息。

客户姓名：{客户姓名}
咨询内容：{咨询内容}
上次沟通：{上次沟通时间}
跟进切入点：{跟进切入点}

要求：
1. 字数40-80字
2. 自然不生硬
3. 提供新的价值信息
4. 不施加压力
5. 适合微信发送

Field Descriptions

FieldDescription
{客户姓名}customer
{咨询内容}before
{上次沟通时间}times is
{跟进切入点}use natural

Output Format

40-80 character follow-up message

Output Example

[Sample output in Chinese - this is a format reference for Chinese-language AI output] 张姐您好～上次您问的那款面霜，这周刚好到了新批次，我留了一份试用装。方便的话可以到店试试，不买也没关系，就当帮我看看效果（仅供格式参考）

The above is a format example only. Actual output should be generated based on your materials.
