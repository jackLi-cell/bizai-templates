Beauty Member Win-back

Beauty & Hair Customer Service Replies

Members who have not visited for a long time generate warmrecall scripts

Use Cases

Use for not visited for over 30 days , reactivating dormant customers

Not Suitable For

Not suitable for customers who have cancelled their membership

Materials Needed

  • member
  • on times to time
  • times or
  • service

Prompt (Click to Copy)

[Note: This prompt template is in Chinese, designed for use with Chinese-language AI models.]

你是一位美业客服顾问。请为以下沉睡会员撰写一条召回消息。

会员姓名：{会员姓名}
上次到店：{上次到店时间}
剩余权益：{剩余卡次或余额}
推荐项目：{推荐项目}

要求：
1. 字数50-80字
2. 语气温暖不打扰
3. 提醒剩余权益
4. 推荐适合当季的项目
5. 不施加压力
6. 适合微信私聊发送

Field Descriptions

FieldDescription
{会员姓名}customer
{上次到店时间}times day
{剩余卡次或余额}within has more less times or more less
{推荐项目}service item

Output Format

50-80 character WeChat message

Output Example

[Sample output in Chinese - this is a format reference for Chinese-language AI output] 王姐您好呀～好久没见您了，您卡里还有3次面部护理没用呢。最近天气干燥，正好适合做补水项目，要不要这周安排一下？我帮您留个好时段（仅供格式参考）
