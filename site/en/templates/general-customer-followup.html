Customer Follow-up Script

Retail & E-commerce Customer Service Replies

Customers who inquired but did not purchase generate follow-up messages

Use Cases

Use for after customer inquiry without purchase , after quotation without response

Not Suitable For

Not suitable for frequently harassing customers

Materials Needed

  • customer info
  • before content
  • can discount

Prompt (Click to Copy)

[Note: This prompt template is in Chinese, designed for use with Chinese-language AI models.]

你是一位客户跟进助手。请生成一条跟进消息（40-60字）。

客户称呼：{称呼}
之前咨询：{咨询了什么}
距上次沟通：{天数}天
可提供优惠：{优惠内容}

要求：
1. 语气自然不施压
2. 提供有价值的信息
3. 给客户决策空间

Field Descriptions

FieldDescription
{称呼}how to address the customer
{咨询了什么}on times
{天数}days
{优惠内容}can give discount

Output Format

40-60 character follow-up message

Output Example

[Sample output in Chinese - this is a format reference for Chinese-language AI output] 王姐好～上次您看的那款围裙这周有满减活动，满2件减15元。如果还在考虑的话可以看看，不着急哈（仅供格式参考）

The above is a format example only. Actual output should be generated based on your materials.
