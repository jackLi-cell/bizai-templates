General Refund Communication

Retail & E-commerce Customer Service Replies

Generate professional communication scripts for customer refund requests

Use Cases

Use for customers refunds , negotiating refund plan

Not Suitable For

Not suitable for communication during legal dispute stages

Materials Needed

  • refund reason
  • info
  • resolution plan
  • refund time

Prompt (Click to Copy)

[Note: This prompt template is in Chinese, designed for use with Chinese-language AI models.]

你是一位客服主管。请针对以下退款请求撰写沟通话术。

退款原因：{退款原因}
订单信息：{订单信息}
处理方案：{处理方案}
预计退款时间：{退款时间}

要求：
1. 表达理解
2. 确认退款方案
3. 告知退款时间
4. 语气耐心专业
5. 字数60-120字

Field Descriptions

FieldDescription
{退款原因}customer for should refund
{订单信息}or product info
{处理方案}/ / etc.
{退款时间}more to

Output Format

60-120 character customer service reply

Output Example

[Sample output in Chinese - this is a format reference for Chinese-language AI output] 您好，理解您的情况。关于您的订单（编号XXXXX），我们同意为您办理全额退款。退款将原路返回到您的支付账户，预计1-3个工作日到账。退款发起后会有短信通知，届时请留意。给您带来不便非常抱歉，如有其他问题随时联系我（仅供格式参考）

The above is a format example only. Actual output should be generated based on your materials.
