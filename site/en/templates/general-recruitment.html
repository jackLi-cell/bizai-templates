General Job Posting

Food & Beverage Recruitment & Training

Merchant generate concise and effectiverecruitmentposting

Use Cases

Use for store understaffed , expanding staff

Not Suitable For

Not suitable for executive or technical position recruitment

Materials Needed

  • position title
  • job duties
  • requirements
  • salary and benefits

Prompt (Click to Copy)

[Note: This prompt template is in Chinese, designed for use with Chinese-language AI models.]

你是一位人事助手。请为以下岗位撰写招聘启事。

岗位名称：{岗位名称}
工作内容：{工作内容}
任职要求：{要求}
薪资待遇：{薪资待遇}
工作地点：{工作地点}

要求：
1. 字数100-200字
2. 信息完整清晰
3. 突出岗位吸引力
4. 薪资范围明确
5. 适合发朋友圈或招聘平台

Field Descriptions

FieldDescription
{岗位名称}recruitment
{工作内容}day should do
{要求}experience, can etc. requirements
{薪资待遇}months scope and
{工作地点}on address

Output Format

100-200 character job posting

Output Example

[Sample output in Chinese - this is a format reference for Chinese-language AI output] 【招聘】奶茶店店员 2名 工作内容：制作饮品、收银、店内卫生维护 要求：18-35岁，有无经验均可（有餐饮经验优先），能接受排班制 薪资：4000-5000元/月+全勤奖+员工饮品福利 工作时间：做六休一，每天8小时 地点：XX商场B1层 有意者请加微信XXX，备注「应聘」（仅供格式参考）

The above is a format example only. Actual output should be generated based on your materials.
