Job Posting Copy

Food & Beverage Recruitment & Training

Merchant write concise and effectiverecruitmentposting

Use Cases

Use for store hiring , seasonal staffing , new store opening recruiting

Not Suitable For

Not suitable for headhunting or executive recruitment

Materials Needed

  • position title
  • job duties
  • salary range
  • working hours
  • contact info

Prompt (Click to Copy)

[Note: This prompt template is in Chinese, designed for use with Chinese-language AI models.]

你是一位招聘文案助手。请为以下岗位撰写招聘启事（80-120字）。

岗位名称：{岗位名称}
工作内容：{主要做什么}
薪资范围：{薪资}
工作时间：{上班时间}
福利待遇：{福利}
联系方式：{联系方式}

要求：
1. 信息完整清晰
2. 突出岗位亮点
3. 不歧视年龄、性别
4. 适合发朋友圈或招聘平台

Field Descriptions

FieldDescription
{岗位名称}position
{主要做什么}day job duties
{薪资}months or scope
{上班时间}working hours arrangement
{福利}etc.
{联系方式}phone or WeChat

Output Format

80-120 character job posting

Output Example

[Sample output in Chinese - this is a format reference for Chinese-language AI output] 【招聘前台收银】工作内容：点单收银、简单清洁。薪资4000-5000/月，做六休一，包午餐。要求：有耐心、会基本手机操作即可。工作地点：XX路XX号。有意者微信联系：XXXXX（仅供格式参考）

The above is a format example only. Actual output should be generated based on your materials.
